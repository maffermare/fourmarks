The Shrine of Sacraments

Reflect on the holiness of the Church through the sacraments.

  1. Write a heartfelt, illustrated "thank you" story about a meaningful experience with the sacraments.
  2. If no such experience has occurred, reflect on grace or forgiveness and create a story based on these themes.

Use your creativity to make this story uplifting and respectful. This is an opportunity to think deeply about how sacraments enrich our lives and connect us to God.
